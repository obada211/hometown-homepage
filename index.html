Top 3 Things to Do in Maaloula

Visit Historic Monasteries

Explore the ancient St. Sergius and Bacchus Monastery, a site of spiritual and architectural beauty.

Hike the Stunning Cliffs

Take a scenic hike through Maaloula's breathtaking cliffs and marvel at the unique rock formations.

Savor Traditional Syrian Cuisine

Indulge in local flavors and try dishes like kibbeh and fresh-baked bread.

Local Culture and Traditions

Hear Aramaic Spoken

Experience one of the few places in the world where Aramaic, the language of Christ, is still spoken.

Join Local Festivals

Celebrate traditional Syrian festivals filled with music, dance, and community spirit.
